Summer Workshops at The Wharf

Sign up for children’s summer workshops and learn about membership options for The Wharf Folk School & Event Center.

Children’s Summer Workshops

Our first two children’s summer workshops will take place in August. Both workshops are for youth ages 6 and up. Complete and print the permission form. Bring the form to the class.

Mural-Making Workshop

Dates: August 10–14
Time: 9:00 AM–1:00 PM
Ages: Youth ages 6 and up

Participants will explore mural painting techniques and experiment with collaborative art-making in a noncompetitive environment, learning about color, composition, and visual storytelling.

Sign Up Permission Form

Theatre Workshop

Dates: August 24–28
Time: 9:00 AM–1:00 PM
Ages: Youth ages 6 and up
Performance: Friday, August 28 at 7:00 PM

Participants will explore Shakespeare in performance, devise an abridged script, and collaboratively produce The Wharf’s first annual Shakespeare in the Park performance series.

The theatre workshop will close with a Friday evening performance at Ranier Park, weather permitting.
